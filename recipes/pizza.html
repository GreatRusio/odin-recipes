Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1(1.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 table spoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees C (190 degrees C). Pat and stretch dough onto a large pizza pan. Cover with desired toppings. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is lightly browned, 20 to 25 minutes.
